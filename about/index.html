About

We build with technical rigour and Indigenous commitment

North Peak Construction Alliance Inc. is an Indigenous-owned construction company supporting public and private clients across Ontario and Canada, with a focus on safety, quality and reliable delivery.

History

A growing company

We are a young, growing company formed by construction professionals. We were founded on a clear conviction: to combine technical delivery capability with authentic Indigenous participation.

This approach lets us bring real value to every project and to the communities where we work.

Construction capability and Indigenous participation, in a single partner.
